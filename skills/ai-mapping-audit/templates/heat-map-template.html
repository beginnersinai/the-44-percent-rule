Why This Map Matters

The 44% Finding

Kim, Kim & Koning (2026) ran a field experiment with 515 startups. They found that the bottleneck to getting value from AI isn't capability — it's discovering where to deploy it.

Companies shown specific examples of how others use AI found 44% more AI use cases, generated 1.9× higher revenue, completed 12% more tasks, were 18% more likely to acquire paying customers, and needed 39.5% less external capital.

This map IS the showing. Every red cell is a place where you've never been shown what AI could do there. Every star is where the research suggests the highest leverage lies. The biggest gains come from product development and strategy — not from content (where most people start).
